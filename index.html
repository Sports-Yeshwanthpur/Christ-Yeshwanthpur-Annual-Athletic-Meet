RULES AND REGULATIONS

Rules for each individual event will be explained by the officials at the venue. The decision of the officials will be final.

All participants are expected to be at the track and field to be able to report to the venue when the announcements are made.

An athlete can take part in any 3 individual events (2 Track Events and 1 Field Event) and in any 1 Relay event.

AND

Individual championships will be decided based on the score obtained from the individual events only.

Name of athletes should be written in block letters.

March past is compulsory for participants. It is mandatory for all the participants to take part in the march past, failing which they will not be allowed to take part in their respective events. The participants, team sports coordinators in their respective uniforms should assemble on the ground.

Inaugural function starts at 9 am on 14th January 2023.
